About Me

My passion for software development started in high school, when a programming course — recommended by my brother — introduced me to the logic and creativity behind code. That early curiosity led me to pursue a degree in Computer Science at George Mason University.

After graduating, I briefly co-led a startup project before the pandemic shut it down. From there, I began my professional journey at Misbar, then joined NurseTim, where I became a core contributor across backend systems. Following its acquisition, I transitioned to Wolters Kluwer, where I now focus on building secure, distributed systems, mentoring junior engineers, and continuously expanding my technical depth.
